My interest in coding began during a high school computer science class. Initially, I enrolled in the class out of curiosity, not knowing what to expect. As we started learning the basics of Java, I was fascinated by how a few lines of code could solve complex problems or create simple games. The logical thinking and creativity involved in writing code appealed to me, and I enjoyed the process of debugging and refining my programs until they worked perfectly. Outside of class, I began experimenting with different programming languages and working on small projects, like building my own calculator or designing simple websites. The more I learned, the more I realized the vast potential of coding, and it became a passion that blended my love for technology and problem-solving.
