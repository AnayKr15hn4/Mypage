I started to like basketball when I was 10, when a close friend invited me to watch a local game. Initially, I was indifferent, but as the game progressed, I found myself captivated by the fast pace, the skillful dribbling, and the intense competition. The energy in the arena was infectious, and I was amazed at the players' athleticism and teamwork. After that experience, I began playing basketball with friends in the neighborhood, practicing my shots and learning the rules. Over time, my appreciation for the sport deepened, and I started following professional games, admiring players who seemed to effortlessly blend strategy, strength, and grace. Basketball became more than just a hobby; it was a passion that brought excitement and joy to my life.
